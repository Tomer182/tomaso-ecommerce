## Mistakes I've Seen People Make

Because we all make mistakes, and that's okay. But you can avoid them:

**Mistake 1: Buying the Cheapest Thing**
Saw a friend buy bulbs for $10 from some Chinese site. After two weeks, half died and the rest work... sort of. Buy known brands‚ÄîPhilips, LIFX, IKEA, TP-Link. Yes, it's more expensive. No, it's not 10x the price.

**Mistake 2: Buying from Every Company**
Suddenly you have Philips, Xiaomi, Samsung, Google... and you're trying to connect everything. It doesn't work well. Choose one ecosystem initially, build your foundation there, and only then start integrating other things.

**Mistake 3: Not Thinking About Internet**
This whole system runs on your network. If your network is slow or unstable‚Äîthis won't work well. I upgraded to a better router, and even added another range extender to my apartment. It's worth it.

**Mistake 4: Doing Everything in One Day**
Been there. Bought a ton of stuff, tried to install everything on one Saturday, and got completely overwhelmed. Do one phase, learn it, then move forward. That's how it stays pleasant and not frustrating.
